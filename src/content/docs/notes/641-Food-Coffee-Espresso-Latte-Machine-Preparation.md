title: Espresso, Latte with Machine Preparation
---

Instructions to prepare espresso, latte with grinder and espresso
machines.

- Put coffee beans in grinder
- Turn on grinder and adjust settings, example 12/16
- Grind beans into holder
- Even out grinds in holder with taper
- Put grinds holder in espresso machine, during preparation, ensure
  pressure gauge around 7-9 by turning handle
- Put shots cup down and press shots button on espresso machine
- Prepare milk and heat with steamer. Steamer done when it sounds like a
  low pitch, similar to an airplane landing sound
- Dump grinds
- Wash:
  - Steamer head and holes if needed
  - Shot output area of espresso machine
  - Milk cup
  - Grinds holder
