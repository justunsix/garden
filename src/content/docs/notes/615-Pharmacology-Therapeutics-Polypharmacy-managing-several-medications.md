title: Polypharmacy and managing several medications
---

Principles Patients and Health Providers should review during medication
use:

1.  Listen with compassion, discussion to patient's results and
    provider's (pharmacist, doctor, nurse) recommendations
2.  Check for interactions with what person is taking, including
    medication, non-prescription drugs, supplements like vitamins,
    minerals, herbal treatment and others. Discuss with doctor if
    anything found
3.  Check dosage is appropriate and discuss with doctor (example blood
    pressure medication)
4.  Assess patient issues and relation to possible side effects (example
    cough with use of ACE inhibitors in certain patients)
5.  Patient understands how to use medication, demonstrate for them
    (example puffers, injections, pre/post instructions for medication
    use)
