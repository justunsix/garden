- Art of Living by Sharon Lebell
- Atomic Habits by James Clear
  - Category: Self-Improvement / Productivity / Behavioral Science
  - Summary: A practical framework for building good habits and breaking
    bad ones through small, consistent changes.
- Better in Every Sense, How the New Science of Sensation Can Help You
  Reclaim Your Life by Norman Farb, PhD & Zindel Segal, PhD
- I Don’t Want to Talk About It: Overcoming the Secret Legacy of Male
  Depression by Terrence Real
  - Category: Mental Health / Psychology / Gender Studies
  - Summary: An in-depth exploration of how hidden depression affects
    men and how they can heal from emotional repression and trauma.
- Man’s Search for Meaning by Viktor Frankl
  - Category: Psychology / Philosophy / Holocaust Memoir
  - Summary: A Holocaust survivor explores how finding meaning in
    suffering is essential to psychological survival and fulfillment.
- Meditations by Marcus Aurelius
  - Category: Philosophy (Stoicism)
  - Summary: Personal reflections from a Roman emperor on
    self-discipline, resilience, and the nature of human life, grounded
    in Stoic philosophy.
- Never Split the Difference by Chris Voss
  - Category: Negotiation / Business / Psychology
  - Summary: A former FBI hostage negotiator reveals counterintuitive
    techniques for high-stakes negotiation that prioritize emotional
    intelligence and tactical empathy.
- Siddhartha by Hermann Hesse
  - Category: Fiction / Spirituality / Philosophy
  - Summary: A spiritual novel following a man’s journey toward
    enlightenment, blending Eastern philosophy with Western literary
    form.
- The Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle
  - Category: Spirituality / Mindfulness
  - Summary: A guide to living in the present moment and transcending
    the ego to achieve inner peace and spiritual awakening.
- The Untethered Soul by Michael A. Singer
  - Category: Spirituality / Personal Growth
  - Summary: A guide to freeing oneself from mental chatter and inner
    limitations by observing thoughts and embracing consciousness.
- The War of Art by Steven Pressfield
  - Category: Creativity / Self-Improvement / Motivation
  - Summary: A motivational manifesto on overcoming resistance and inner
    blocks to unlock creative potential and pursue meaningful work.
