## Connecting Apple Thunderbolt Display (2011-2016) to Thunderbolt 3 Devices.

An [Apple Thunderbolt
Display](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Apple_Thunderbolt_Display) (a
27-inch flat panel computer monitor developed by Apple and sold from
July 2011 to June 2016) and can be connected to Mac computers with
Thunderbolt 3 such as Macbooks in 2026. The display uses an older
Thunderbolt 2 and using an adapter, can connect to Apple Thunderbolt 3
(USB C) such as an Apple laptop.

The adapter is called the [Thunderbolt 3 (USB C) to Thunderbolt 2
Adapter](https://www.apple.com/ca/shop/product/myh93am/a/thunderbolt-3-usb%E2%80%91c-to-thunderbolt-2-adapter).
It allows connections to the Thunderbolt 3 device as a dock and monitor.
Note, Thunderbolt 2 is not just display and will not connect properly as
just a display to other USB C enabled personal computers.
