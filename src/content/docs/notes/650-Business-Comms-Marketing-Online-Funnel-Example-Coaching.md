title: Coaching Online Funnel Example

Example based on a Rust language learning website and YouTube channel.

- Create free content online and examples, Videos, Website
- Create free email gated training videos, for example 4 videos acrosss
  4 weeks
  - Describe intended audience
  - In each video page, promote direct coaching and learning progression
    and tips to help learners
- Offer limited time strategy sessions using a questionnaire
