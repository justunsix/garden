title: Digital Marketing Communication Survey for Organizations
---

The goal of this assessment is to collect information from organizations
in the middle of marketing communications campaigns.

The assessment aims to get:

- Ideas the organization may have heard from their clients (target
  audience of the campaigns)
- Changes required to the campaigns, for example due to seasonal or
  product and service changes
- Feedback on campaigns: what to start, what to stop or reduce, what to
  keep the same
- Impacts the organization has seen during the campaign period
- Additional input to campaign execution like advertising keywords,
  branding, new products/services to promote, deals

Concepts for organization to explore

- Results seen from the organization (what they see, hear, problems)
- Changes required (seasonal, budget, communication approach, product
  services) and dates
- Start (new keywords, messaging), stop (under performing channels)
- Opportunities for next 3 months
