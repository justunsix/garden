## Toilet Repairs

- Regular home maintenance recommend turn water valve at least once per
  year

Concepts

- Input: clean water
- Output: only toilet paper, dirty water, body fluids
  - No wipes, solids
  - With children, pets: keep lid down, protect against things falling,
    like toys

### Troubleshooting problems

- An approach: Observe a good working toilet in home and the one causing
  problem and look for differences

#### Plugged up

Options:

- Plunging: prefer elongated toilet plunger, not traditional one
  - Be aggressive with plunging like CPR
  - Put plunger through a hole in garbage bag and put bag over toilet to
    make clean up easier
- Snake aka augur
  - Needs to be specifically for use with a toilet
  - Wrap snake with a pool noodle / cloth to avoid damaging toilet bowl
- Dawn dish soap
  - Used to break down stuff
  - Pour 1 cup and let sit 1 hour, pour warm water into toilet, Resume
    plunging if needed

### Leaks

- Check base of take, base of toilet overflow tank, water shut off valve
- Recommend: avoid caulk around toilet to see issues when they occur

### Water Level Stains / Build up

- Use paper nail file, emory board. Do not use pumice stone / hard
  material that can damage bowl
- If toilet is low flow, can be due to mineral deposits blocking holes,
  use vinegar or CLR to clean holes
- Recommend: no tank/bowl hangovers

### Constantly Filling Tank

- Caused by water Leaking Tank to Bowl
- Check flapper in tank, if needing replacement:
  - Stop water
  - Flush
  - Extract flapper and take to store to buy new one
