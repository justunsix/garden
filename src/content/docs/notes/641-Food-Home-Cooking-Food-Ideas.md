## Beef / Pork

- Pizza
- Sausage and buns, sliced tomatoes
- Meatloaf, carrots, mashed potates or rice
- Macronini, ground meat with celery or tomatoes
- Beef burgers with buns, lightly fried onions, slided tomatoes, mustard
- Ribs with rice, potatoes, Salad with potato/beet/other salad
- Vegetable or meat dumplings with salad or salad green in dumpling soup
- Pork chop, sweet potato and brown rice, cauliflower
- Dim sum: BBQ pork bun, bean curd rolls, boiled vegetables, BBQ pork
  rice noodle, Beef stomach (tripe), Chicken feet

## Chicken

- Flattened chicken, cauliflower with mushroom soup sauce
- Chicken Parmesean with pasta and mushrooms
- Pad Thai
- Pineapple Fried Rice with chicken and other ingredients

## Chicken - Eggs

- 2 eggs, half avocado, 2 cups of either kale, spinach, broccoli,
  peppers, asparagus and brown or white rice or grilled chopped sweet
  potato
  - Portions: 1/4 plate is eggs and avocado, 1/2 plate is vegetables,
    1/4 plate is carbohydrates

## Turkey

- Turkey (5kg / 11 pounds) small size with stuffing:
  - Onions, mushrooms
  - Gravy
  - Chicken broth

## Vegetarian

- Macaroni and cheese with vegetables (Brussels sprouts, cauliflower,
  broccoli), avocado
- Pasta / ravioli / perogies / dumplings with vegetables

## Seafood

- Eel friend rice
