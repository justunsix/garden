Lesson plan appropriate for children aged 3-5 with excellent listening
skills and good strength, flexibility, and discipline.

Plans for Artistic and Trampoline Gymnastics

## Coaching Point

Audible, Visual, and Clear and Consistent way to get class attention to
focus on you

E.g. "eyes on me and here we go" with point to eyes motions and dive

## Advanced Kinder — June 2017 with Coach PB

### Warm Up (20-25m)

#### RUNNING WARM UP

Use two strips of the floor

Make sure to ask them to "Show me you're ready" before each exercise —
they're ready when they are in a single file line with ballet arms at
their sides.

| Exercise | Details |
|----|----|
| Running (5x) | Have them high five you every time they run down the first strip |
| Running with knees up |  |
| Running and kicking bum |  |
| Frog hops |  |
| Long jumps |  |
| Bunny hops |  |
| One foot hopping | Hop on first foot on the way there, and on the second foot on the way back |
| Marching | One hand to their ear, one hand to their hip as they march. The elbow in front should be the opposite of the knee up. After marching down the first strip, they run back to the beginning. |
| Front kicks | After front kicks down the first strip, they run back to the beginning |

They may take a drink after this and expected to come back to the floor
and put their nose on the line.

#### CONDITIONING

| Exercise            | Duration / Reps | Details                                |
|---------------------|-----------------|----------------------------------------|
| Push ups            | 10x             | They try to get their nose to the line |
| Hollow body rockers | 20 seconds      |                                        |
| Arch rockers        | 20 seconds      |                                        |
| Clear support       | 3 x 10 seconds  |                                        |
| Wall sit            | 30 seconds      |                                        |

#### STRETCH

Similar to a kinder stretch

Make sure to include pike sit with toes pointed, flexed and arched — 20
seconds each

Run your hand under their heels for pointed and flexed to see if it will
fit.

### Rotations (15-20m each)

If you notice them doing something particularly well, you can reward
them with an extra stamp. They'll work really hard for this, so if you
find they're being sloppy, remind them you're looking to give out extra
stamps.

Have them sit in pike while you're giving instructions and setting up.

#### Floor

1.  Circuit (10-15 minutes)

    | Setup                            | Skill                                    |
    |----------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
    | Blue cheese                      | Back roll to feet                        |
    | Two Velcro lines                 | Handstands                               |
    | Mushroom + large red mat         | Fall to back                             |
    | Two blocks + a line between them | Cartwheel between the blocks             |
    | Red mat                          | Front roll, stand up without using hands |

2.  Stations — two people per station (5-10 minutes)

    | Setup | Skill | Notes |
    |----|----|----|
    | Mini-cheese against the wall | Back bend with feet on floor, hands on cheese | With spot if necessary; switch after group has done 3 |
    | Cheese standing up against the wall | Kick up to handstand |  |
    | Two stacked blocks | Handstand against the block, bent at the hips with knees on the block | Focusing on shrugging shoulders and pushing armpits into the block |

#### Beam

Hold releve (tip toes) on the low beam (30 seconds)

Shoulders should be back, stomach should be tight, knees should be
straight

1.  Circuit — low beams (10 minutes)

    | Activity                                               |
    |--------------------------------------------------------|
    | Bear walking with straight knees                       |
    | Backwards tip toe walking, sliding feet along the edge |
    | Handstands with a beam pad (with spot)                 |

2.  Circuit — high beams (10 minutes)

    | Activity                                           |
    |----------------------------------------------------|
    | Dismounts (stuck): Straight, Star, Straddle, Pike  |
    | Handstand walking with feet along the GymNova beam |

    While they're waiting to do their dismounts, you can put down the
    pink baby beam for them to sit on.

#### Vault

1.  Circuit (15 minutes)

    | Setup | Skill |
    |----|----|
    | Blue block + blue crash mat | Handstand, fall to back |
    | Long grey beatboard + blue block (halfway up the back of the beatboard) + orange square mat | Step off the block, arm circle, jump on beatboard, land on mat with arms up |
    | Tumbl trak | Spider hops |

    If you need to use the floor rather than vault, you can replace this
    with running long jump — have them jump onto the small red mat.

#### Bars

1.  Conditioning (5 minutes)

    | Exercise  | Duration               |
    |-----------|------------------------|
    | Pike hold | 20 seconds, 15 seconds |

    Split them up so that two kids are on the single wooden bar, one on
    each of the yellow bars, and two on the low bar.

2.  Stations — two people per station (15 minutes)

    | Station | Skill | Notes |
    |----|----|----|
    | Low bar (competitive bar set) | Swings (x3) | Switch stations once each person has had two turns |
    | Red bars | Tuck hold |  |
    | Yellow bars | Casts, roll down forward |  |

### Cool Down (5-10m)

#### SPLITS

| Split     | Duration |
|-----------|----------|
| Right leg | 1 minute |
| Left leg  | 1 minute |

#### GAME — If there's time left over

"Reindeer Game"

- Put a block in the middle of two strips on the floor
- One person is It, they count to 10 before chasing everyone around the
  block in one direction
- When someone is caught, they sit on the block
- Whoever is caught last can be It next

#### PRESENT

- Have them line up on the pink floor and present to you individually
  - Feet together, tummy tight, shoulders down and back, chin up
- After they present, you can give them their stamps
- When you give extra stamps, ask them to tell you why they are getting
  an extra stamp
