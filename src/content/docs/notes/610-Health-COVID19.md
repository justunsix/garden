## Gradual Return of Work (Example Approach)

Target return Sept or Oct 2021 return. 2-3 days / week

Why return to work?

- Work has the right and believes it is safe with precautions (capacity,
  gradual return)
- Many employees remained in the workplace during pandemic

Vaccination policy?

- Hospitals and schools led with vaccination priority policy. People
  without vaccination will be tested. Goal is to increase total
  vaccinations.
- Medical exemptions only and choice is available
- Testing:
  - Regular for days you are in office
  - On employer's time and cost
  - Failed test policy to follow standard sick processes
