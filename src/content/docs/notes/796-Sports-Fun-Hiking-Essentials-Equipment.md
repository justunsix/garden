## My 10 Essentials From About.com

### 1. Navigation

- Map (+ case)
- Compass
- Optional: Altimeter

### 2. Sun Protection

- Sunscreen
- Sunglasses (polarized)

### 3. Insulation

- Additional layer of clothing
- Optional: Head covers

### 4. Illumination

- Headlamp (or flashlight)

### 5. First-aid Supplies

- First aid kit (treatments for blisters, adhesive bandages of various
  sizes, several gauze pads, adhesive tape, disinfecting ointment,
  over-the-counter pain medication, pencil and paper. Latex gloves also
  deserve consideration + first aid guide). Optional: ear plugs

### 6. Fire

- Matches in case
- Firestarter (candle, lint).

### 7. Repair Kit and Tools

- Rescue tool
- Duct tape

### 8. Nutrition (extra food)

- Extra food(energy bars, nuts, dried fruits or jerky)

### 9. Hydration (extra water)

- Water bottle(s)
- Water carrier

### 10. Emergency Shelter

- Tarp
- Space blanket
- Garbage bag large and bright colour

### Beyond the Top Ten

- Insect repellent:
- Whistle
- Ice axe: For safety when crossing snow fields.
- Communication device: Two-way radios, a cell phone or a satellite
  telephone
- Signalling device: space blanket, compass mirror
