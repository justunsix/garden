# Dental Clinics in Toronto Accepting Patients - University of Toronto

See [Patient Information \| Patients at the Faculty of Dentistry Clinics
(University of Toronto)](https://patients.dentistry.utoronto.ca/)

Variety of clinics to choose with reduced fees, coverage, and
appointment booking.

Services include general dentistry, child, emergency dentistry,
anaesthesia, orthodontics, prosthodonctics, periodontics, oral surgery,
oral pathology and others.
