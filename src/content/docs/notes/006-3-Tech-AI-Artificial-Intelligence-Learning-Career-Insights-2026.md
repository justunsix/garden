### Reasons to get into the industry

Advantage of AI is using machines to simulate human capabilities using
data and at a large connected scale.

AI likely will affect all parts of society including jobs.

## Importance of Communication

Once you have technical skills, it is important to have and practice
communication skills as person to advance your career and explain proper
use of AI and your technical skills. Examples of practice are
presentations.

For communication and work in data, remember the audience (who), purpose
(why), and outcome (results).

## Advice on Jobs

Instead of talking: show proof of work, for example a portfolio on
resume, online profile

Start small: look at data, tell a story, for example government open
data and organization's data. For example, start a research project or
event and interview/invite relevant people. Complete it and add it to
your portfolio and connect with those people.

Networking and coffee chats are more focused than just sending resumes.
Provide value to the person you met. Think it is like planting seeds and
results come later and not all seeds give fruit.

## Looking at Change due to Artificial Intelligence (AI)

It is opportunity; however, all jobs could be impacted. Good to invest
in yourself like soft and business skills.

Mindset change: Embrace technology and ask how you can solve a problem
using tools available.
