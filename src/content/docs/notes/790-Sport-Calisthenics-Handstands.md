## Handstand Classes 2022-01-09

- Warm up 1
  - 10 jumping jacks
  - 5 lunge twists each side
  - 10 each side Greatest stretch of all time (GSOAT) - lunge, reach to
    ground, reach to sky
  - 10 squats
  - Repeat continuously for 2 mins
- Warm up 2
  - 10 shoulder flosses
  - 20 hollow boat rocks
  - 10 scapulas push-ups
  - 5 airplanes each side
  - Repeat continuously for 2 mins
- Stretching
- Backbend, handstand exercises

## Handstand Classes 2021-08-15

### Warm Up

- Jogging
- Joint rotations
- Jumping Jacks
- Inch worms
- Stretching

### Splits

- Pyramid fold to lunges
- Back leg raises
  - 10 lifts on each side
  - Hold 10 seconds
- Splits
  - Isometrics
  - Full split on each side and side splits

### Bridges

- Prone, turtle back
- Puppy dog stretch on wall + isometric
- 30-60s handstands straight
- handstands straddle
- Scorpion stretches
- Quad stretch (bum, leg against wall), knee on floor
- 5 x bridge + number 4 cross leg stretch and plow pose

## Handstand Classes 2021-04/05

### Warmup

1.  Dance / running in place
2.  Hip circles, joint rotations
3.  Jumping jacks (high/low impact)
4.  Inch worms in place
5.  Full body stretching
    1.  Runner's lunge, pyramid fold, hamstring curl hold
    2.  1 leg kick ups

### Core 1

1.  40 sec handstand hold
2.  Hollow body hold (lower back stay on floor), tuck if necessary
3.  2 x Bicep curl with book in table top belly facing down, 3 kick ups
    on 1 leg

### Core 2

1.  Shoulder shrugs x 3 - downward dog, high plank, plank
2.  Finger push ups in table top belly down, kneeling plank
3.  Hollow body holds
4.  Hollow body sideways rocks

### Finisher

1.  T holds handstands (1 foot on wall, other leg 90 degrees from wall)
2.  Child's pose arm lifts x 5, forehead to ground
3.  2 x tuck jumps + Superman to lat pull down x 5
